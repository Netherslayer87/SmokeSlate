Beat Saber Modding Assistant

Overview

This Apple Shortcut streamlines the process of modding the popular VR game, Beat Saber. It automates the downloading, organizing, and installation of custom mods, ensuring a seamless gaming experience.

Features

  • Automated Mod Download: Quickly access the latest mods from trusted sources with a single tap.
  • Organized Mod Storage: Automatically sort and store downloaded mods in designated folders for easy management.
  • Easy Installation: Install mods directly into your Beat Saber game with minimal effort.
  • Backup and Restore: Create backups of your current mods and game settings, and restore them anytime.

Requirements

  • iOS device with the Apple Shortcuts app installed.
  • Beat Saber game installed on your VR setup.
  • An active internet connection for downloading mods.

Installation

  1. Download the Beat Saber Modding Assistant shortcut from the Apple Shortcuts Gallery.
  2. Open the Shortcuts app and add the downloaded shortcut.
  3. Customize any settings or preferences to match your setup.
  4. Run the shortcut and follow the on-screen instructions.

Usage

  1. Launch the Beat Saber Modding Assistant from the Shortcuts app or your home screen.
  2. Select the mods you want to download and install.
  3. Follow the prompts to complete the installation process.
  4. Enjoy your enhanced Beat Saber experience with new mods!
